Work, Material Created

I made designs for the following: main and alternative logo, stickers, clothing, buisness card, signage, courtesy mats

Concept

EMG is a shop that specialzies in high end European vehicle repair and modification. The founders both got their careers started with BMW, and have a lot of BMW clientele. The main requirement was something simple and to the point.

With the above in mind, I decided to create a logo that is primarily a word mark. The sans-serifs are all slightly heavier weights so that they stand out easier when displayed on a vehicle. On the EMG abbreviated logo, the top bars on the E, and the G's bar both are rounded for visual interest.

The stripes, which by default are reminiscent of the BMW Motorsports colors, add both visual interest and some brand coloring. The stripe colors can be changed for different marques, occasions (i.e. for a special run of stickers), etc.

Reflection

The EMG job was slightly more expedited than usual, but Michael and Josh were excellent to work with. We iterated over 3 designs, with the current one being the 3rd and final.

Overall, I'm pleased with the work, and they were as well.
